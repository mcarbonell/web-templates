The Challenge

Outdated Systems, Missed Opportunities

Our client, a major retailer with over 500 physical locations, was struggling with an outdated e-commerce platform that couldn't keep up with modern consumer expectations. The legacy system was slow, difficult to navigate, and lacked mobile optimization.

Key challenges included declining online sales, high cart abandonment rates, poor mobile conversion, and inability to personalize the shopping experience at scale.

  • 70% cart abandonment rate
  • 3+ second page load times
  • Mobile conversion under 1%

The Solution

A Complete Digital Overhaul

We designed and built a new e-commerce platform from the ground up, focusing on speed, user experience, and scalability. The solution included a headless commerce architecture, AI-powered personalization, and a mobile-first design approach.

Key features implemented included real-time inventory management, one-click checkout, personalized product recommendations, and seamless omnichannel integration.

  • Headless commerce architecture
  • AI-powered personalization engine
  • Sub-second page load times
  • Omnichannel integration
