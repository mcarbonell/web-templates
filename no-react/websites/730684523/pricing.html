FAQ

Pricing Questions

What's included in the project price?

Our project prices include all design work, development, testing, and launch support. You'll receive all source files and documentation. Hosting and domain costs are separate.

Can I upgrade my plan mid-project?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll simply adjust the scope and pricing for the remaining work. Many clients start with Starter and upgrade as their needs grow.

Do you offer payment plans?

Absolutely. We typically structure payments as 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint, and 25% upon completion. For larger projects, we can create custom milestone-based payment schedules.

What if I need ongoing support?

We offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing support, maintenance, and continuous improvement. These start at $2,000/month and can be customized to your needs.
