Our Story

More Than Just Things

Grey Mercantile began in a small garage in Portland's Alberta Arts District. What started as a passion project between two friends has grown into a community of makers, dreamers, and collectors who believe that the objects we surround ourselves with should tell a story.

Every piece in our collection is chosen not just for its beauty, but for the hands that made it, the materials that form it, and the intention behind it. We believe in slow commerce, ethical sourcing, and supporting independent artisans around the world.
