The Challenge

AutoCo faced increasing pressure from investors to decarbonize its supply chain. With over 12,000 suppliers globally, manual spreadsheets were impossible to manage.

The Solution

Using EcoStream's Supply Chain module, AutoCo automated data collection, standardizing emission factors across all tier-1 and tier-2 suppliers. The platform identified high-impact areas immediately.

"EcoStream gave us the visibility we didn't think was possible for another decade."

The Results

Beyond the 40% reduction, AutoCo now has a transparent, audit-ready dataset that has improved their ESG rating and lowered their cost of capital.
