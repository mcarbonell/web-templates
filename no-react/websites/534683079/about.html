ABOUT US

Building the Future of Work

We're on a mission to make collaboration effortless for distributed teams around the world.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FlowSync was born from a simple observation: remote work tools were fragmented, complicated, and didn't reflect how teams actually work.

Our founders, having experienced the challenges of distributed collaboration firsthand, set out to create a platform that would feel natural, powerful, and delightful to use.

Today, we serve over 50,000 teams across 150 countries, helping them collaborate more effectively than ever before.
