About Us

We're More Than Just Photographers

Founded in 2015, Lumina Shutter Studios has grown from a small passion project to a full-service photography and videography studio. We believe that every moment has a story worth telling, and we're here to tell it with authenticity and artistry.

Our team of 5 professional photographers and videographers brings diverse perspectives and specialties to every project. We use state-of-the-art equipment and post-production techniques to ensure your visuals are nothing short of spectacular.
