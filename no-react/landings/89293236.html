Our Story

Rooted in passion, grown with care

Vale Studio began with a simple belief: exceptional food brings people together. Founded in 2015 by Chef Elena Valesquez, we've spent nearly a decade perfecting the art of event catering.

Our team sources ingredients from local farms within 50 miles, ensuring every dish tells a story of our region's bounty. From the first consultation to the final bite, we're dedicated to creating experiences that exceed expectations.
