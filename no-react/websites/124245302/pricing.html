FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the brand guidelines?

Our brand guidelines include logo usage rules, color palettes with exact values, typography specifications, imagery guidelines, tone of voice, and application examples across various touchpoints.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A Starter package typically takes 2-3 weeks, while Professional projects range from 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Can I upgrade my plan mid-project?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll simply adjust the scope and timeline accordingly. Any work already completed will be credited toward your new plan.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all files in multiple formats including vector (AI, EPS, SVG), raster (PNG, JPG), and source files. We ensure you have everything needed for any application.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically, we require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remainder due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

What if I'm not satisfied with the designs?

We work collaboratively throughout the process to ensure alignment. With unlimited revisions on Professional and Enterprise plans, we keep refining until you're completely satisfied.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help you choose the right plan for your needs.
