Our Story

Brewing Excellence Since 2019

What started as a homebrewing passion between three friends has grown into one of Portland's most beloved craft breweries. We believe that great beer brings people together.

At HOPSCRAFT, every batch is a labor of love. Our head brewer, Sarah Chen, brings 15 years of experience from some of Europe's finest breweries, while our taproom team creates an atmosphere that's welcoming to beer novices and connoisseurs alike.

  • 100% independent and locally owned
  • Sustainable brewing practices
  • Community-focused events and partnerships
  • Award-winning beer selection
