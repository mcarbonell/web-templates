About Us

Where Healing Meets Science

At Lumina Room, we believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. Our team of licensed physiotherapists combines cutting-edge techniques with compassionate care to deliver results that last.

Founded in 2009, we've helped thousands of patients overcome pain, recover from injuries, and achieve their physical goals. Our evidence-based approach ensures you receive the most effective treatment available.
