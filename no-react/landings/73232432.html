FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aurora.

What makes Aurora different from other design tools? +

Aurora combines the power of professional design software with the simplicity of modern web apps. Our unique approach focuses on removing friction from the creative process while providing advanced features when you need them. Plus, our real-time collaboration engine is built from the ground up for speed and reliability.

Can I use Aurora for commercial projects? +

Absolutely! All plans, including the free Starter plan, allow for commercial use. You retain full ownership of everything you create in Aurora. For enterprise customers, we also offer additional licensing options and custom agreements.

Is there a limit to how many team members I can invite? +

Starter plans are limited to individual use. Pro plans support up to 10 team members, and Enterprise plans have no limits on team size. All collaborators get full access to real-time editing, comments, and version history.

What file formats can I export to? +

Starter users can export to PNG, JPG, and PDF. Pro and Enterprise users get access to SVG, EPS, PSD layered files, and even production-ready code exports including React, Vue, and vanilla HTML/CSS components.

Do you offer educational discounts? +

Yes! We offer 50% off for students and educators. We also have special programs for educational institutions. Contact our education team to learn more about our classroom and campus-wide licensing options.
