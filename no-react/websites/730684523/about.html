Our Story

From Garage to Global

Founded in 2009 in a small garage in Brooklyn, Nexus Creative began with a simple belief: that great design can change the world. What started as three friends with a laptop and a dream has grown into an award-winning agency with a global footprint.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of working with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between. Each project has taught us something new, shaped our approach, and reinforced our commitment to excellence.

Today, we're a team of 40+ designers, developers, strategists, and storytellers united by a common purpose: to create work that matters.
