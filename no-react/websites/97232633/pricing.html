Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing and packages.

All packages include design, development, testing, and deployment. Professional and Enterprise plans also include CMS setup, SEO optimization, and post-launch support. Hosting and domain costs are separate.

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers, credit cards, and PayPal.

All packages include a support period where I fix any bugs or issues at no extra cost. After the support period, I offer maintenance packages or can handle changes at my standard hourly rate.

I offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the initial design concepts, you can request a full refund before development begins. Once development starts, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.
