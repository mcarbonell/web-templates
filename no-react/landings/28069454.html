About Prime Lounge

A neighbourhood barbershop with a focus on craft and comfort.

We opened Prime Lounge to bring back the experience of a real barbershop: skilled hands, honest conversation, and a place to slow down. Our team has years of experience in cuts, fades, and classic grooming.

Whether you're in for a quick trim or the full hot-towel treatment, we've got you. Walk-ins welcome; appointments recommended for weekends.
