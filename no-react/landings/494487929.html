About BalanceCare

Healing with Heart and Expertise

Founded in 2009, BalanceCare Chiropractic has been serving our community with compassionate, evidence-based care. Our mission is simple: to help you achieve optimal health and wellness through natural, non-invasive treatments.

Led by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a board-certified chiropractor with advanced training in sports medicine and pediatric care, our team combines traditional chiropractic techniques with modern approaches to deliver exceptional results.
