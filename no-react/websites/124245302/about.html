Our Journey

From small beginnings to creative excellence

Founded in 2014, Nova Creative Studio began with a simple mission: to help businesses tell their stories through compelling design and strategic thinking. What started as a two-person operation has grown into a full-service creative agency with a global client base.

Over the past 12 years, we've had the privilege of working with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between. Each project has taught us something new and shaped our approach to creative problem-solving.

Today, our team of 25+ creatives, strategists, and developers work collaboratively to deliver experiences that not only look beautiful but drive measurable business results.
